About Us

We're a group of students based on the Royal Technical University (KTH) in Stockholm, Sweden. After observing the hassle of many trading sites where you have to register and enter a lot of data to sell or buy your stuff, we decided to create a simple trading site to allow people to sell and buy items fast and easily.
